# Image Guide for Home Page

## Hero Section Image

**File Location:** `/static/images/hero-education.jpg`

**Purpose:** This image appears in the hero section on the home page (right side, next to the headline).

### Recommended Image:
- **Theme:** Children learning, studying, reading books, or in a classroom setting
- **Feeling:** Hopeful, educational, inspiring
- **Content:** Should represent free education for underprivileged children
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Recommended Size:** 800x600px or larger (aspect ratio ~4:3)
- **Quality:** High resolution for best appearance

### Suggested Image Ideas:
- Children reading books together
- Students in a classroom with books
- Teacher helping children learn
- Children writing or studying
- Group of diverse children with educational materials
- Children with backpacks and books heading to school

### Free Image Resources:
- Unsplash: Search "children education learning"
- Pexels: Search "children school education"
- Pixabay: Search "children studying"

### Fallback:
If the image file doesn't exist, the page will automatically use an Unsplash placeholder image of children learning.

### To Update:
1. Find a relevant education-themed image
2. Save it as `hero-education.jpg` in `/static/images/` directory
3. Refresh your browser to see the update
